HOW TO APPLY FOR SCHOLARSHIP?

Here are the REQUIREMENTS!

📌 Certified true copy of Official Transcript of record (TOR)

📌 Certified true copy of Form 137 (Permanent Record if the candidates previous education is Elementary or Secondary Level)

📌 PSA birth certificate (xerox copy)

📌 Certificate of Marriage (candidates prior to or after enrollment)

📌 Certificate of Indigency

📌 1x1 ID picture with name tag, with collar and white background (studio type)

📌 Passport size ID picture with name tag, with collar and white background (studio type)


Beneficiaries should be: 1. Filipino Citizen; 2. Unemployed / underemployed 3. at least 18 years of age at the end of training


WE are on a FIRST COME and TO COMPLY, FIRST SERVED BASIS‼️
